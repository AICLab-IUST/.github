<div align="center"> 
The Artificial Intelligence and Control Laboratory conducts research on AI‑based and data‑driven control, reinforcement learning, autonomous robotics, navigation, and optimal control with strong mathematical foundations.
</div>

<div align="center"> 
The lab develops theoretical and applied research projects and actively publishes in peer‑reviewed international journals and conferences. It also offers undergraduate and graduate‑level courses, including Linear Control Systems, Principles of Mechatronics, Reinforcement Learning in Control, Model Predictive Control, and Convex Optimization.
</div>

<div align="center"> 
Course‑specific repositories and projects are organized under dedicated sub‑organizations for <a href="https://github.com/RLinControl">Reinforcement Learning in Control</a></em> and <a href="https://github.com/LinearControlSystems">Linear Control Systems</a></em>.
</div>
